Code of Conduct

G. NOVA ARENA aims to create a safe, respectful, and competitive environment across all gaming and sports events. General Expectations . Treat all participants, organisers, and officials with respect. . Follow event rules and guidelines as provided. Online Events . No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics. . Disputes will be resolved by organisers. All decisions are final. Offline Events . Any injuries will be escorted to the hospital, but G. NOVA ARENA is not responsible for treatment costs. . Players participate at their own risk. G. NOVA ARENA shall not be held liable for any fatal injuries or deaths occurring during offline events. Betting Policy Our organisation strictly does not promote, encourage, or support any form of betting or gambling. Any individual or group found engaging in betting activities within our organisation’s events, or misusing our organisation’s name to conduct or promote betting, will face strict legal action. Health & Safety Disclaimer Participants are responsible for ensuring they are medically fit to take part in our offline sports tournaments. Individuals with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart disease or other serious medical issues, are strongly advised to refrain from participation. Our organisation will not be held liable for any health-related incidents, injuries, or complications that may arise during participation. Disciplinary Actions Violation of the Code of Conduct may lead to: . Disqualification from current and future events . Banning from the community . Legal action in extreme cases
