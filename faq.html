Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is G. NOVA ARENA?
G. NOVA ARENA is a hybrid gaming and sports community that organises competitive online tournaments and offline events across football, basketball, cricket, and more. We bring together digital gamers and field athletes under one arena, fostering skill, sportsmanship, and community engagement.
Q2: Who can participate in your events?
Anyone aged 18 or above can register and participate in our events.
Q3: Do I need to create an account to join events?
No. Our website is partially static and does not support user account creation. All registrations are handled through Google Forms.
Q4: How do I register for an event?
Click the Register button on the event page. It will redirect you to a Google Form where you must fill in your details and upload payment proof.
Q5: What information do I need to provide while registering?
Name, age, gender, email, phone number or WhatsApp number, and payment proof.
Q6: Where do your offline events happen?
At rented stadiums, clubs, or grounds. Details are mentioned in each event's Google Form.
Q7: What platforms are used for online tournaments?
We host online gaming events on popular platforms and game titles. Specifics are mentioned in the event details.
Q8: How do I pay the entry fee?
Payments are made via a UPI QR code included in the Google Form. We do not use any payment gateway on our website.
Q9: Is my registration confirmed after payment?
Yes — once you submit the form and upload payment proof, your registration is considered complete.
Q10: Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
Refunds are only offered if an event doesn’t meet the minimum team requirement. No refunds for cancellations or no-shows.
Q11: Do you sell or share my personal data?
No. We do not sell or share your data. It is securely stored in our Google account environment.
Q12: Are cookies used on this website?
No. Our website does not use any cookies or tracking technologies.
Q13: Are there rules for online events?
Yes. Cheating or exploiting in online events is strictly prohibited. All decisions made by the organisers are final.
Q14: What if I get injured during an offline event?
We assist in getting the injured participant to medical care, but we are not liable for treatment or hospital charges. Participation is at your own risk.
Q15: What kind of behavior is expected at events?
We expect fair play, respect, and discipline. Any violation, including rough play or misconduct, may lead to disqualification or a permanent ban.
Q16: Does G. NOVA ARENA allow betting or gambling?
No, it is strictly prohibited.
Q17: Can people with health issues join offline sports?
Participation is voluntary; individuals with health concerns should avoid joining.
